Who Am I?

I am a student studying Cybersecurity at Grand Valley State University.

I am also employed as a Jr. IT Security Specialist at a software development firm and independent software vendor for wholesale greenhouses called The Picas Group.

At The Picas Group, I have gained experience in both supporting an in-house software and providing managed services, such as Web-Application Firewalls, IT, and Cloud Hosting with Microsoft Azure.

What Are My Security Passions?

One of my favorite concepts in Cybersecurity at this point in my education is encryption. I have always enjoyed math from the time I was a little kid until now, and I find the mathematical concepts used in modern encryption to be fascinating.

Another passion of mine in terms of security is web-application security. This was introduced to me when I began working at The Picas Group. Some of my favorite security related tasks that I have done at The Picas Group was implementing Web-Application Firewalls, securing admin pages to some of our applications, and implementing HTTPS certificates.

I also have enjoyed working with our SentinelOne tenant and Elastic's Cloud offering and familiarizing myself with their query languages, as well as implementing custom detection rules in both consoles.
